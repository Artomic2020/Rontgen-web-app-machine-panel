What is this all about?

This usability and concept test focuses on (PWA) machine-panel dashboard prototype, its screen tools and (UI) functionality.

The purpose of usability testing is to collect data and insights about the functionality, user experience (UX) and user-interface (UI) design.

This project is currently in development and early release of details can distort or impede our results. As it is ongoing research, you are asked to abide by a non-disclosure agreement.

Your answers and insights will be handled in a way that protect against the misuse of personal data. Any information you choose to provide in the course of the study, such as address, operating system, e-mail, phone number, will NOT presented publicly with the results of the surveys and interviews (unless separately specified).

Your participation is voluntary and the insights and information you provide is also shared on a voluntary basis. You are free to withdraw from the study at any time. Nothing in this UX/UI usability and concept test makes you (or precludes you) an employee or partner of the project. All rights are reserved by Artomic Design.
